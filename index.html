Admissions

Enrollment Process

  1. Step 1: Online Application

    Fill out the online application form with required details and documents.

  2. Step 2: Entrance Examination

    Take the entrance exam based on your grade level to assess academic readiness.

  3. Step 3: Personal Interview

    Attend an interview with our admissions team and school administration.

  4. Step 4: Document Verification

    Submit all required documents for verification including certificates and transcripts.

  5. Step 5: Final Confirmation

    Receive admission confirmation and complete fee payment to secure admission.

Frequently Asked Questions

Sunrise Academy believes in student-centric, activity-based learning. We focus on developing critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills alongside academics. Our philosophy emphasizes holistic development and preparing students for real-world challenges.

We maintain a student-to-teacher ratio of 20:1 in primary classes and 25:1 in secondary classes. This ensures personalized attention and effective learning for each student.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships up to 50% for outstanding students and need-based scholarships for deserving candidates. Students can apply during the admission process.

We offer a wide range of activities including sports, music, dance, drama, art, debate, science clubs, community service, and coding. Students are encouraged to participate in at least two activities.

We follow the principle of quality over quantity. Homework is given to reinforce learning and develop self-discipline. However, we ensure it doesn't become a burden and allow time for play and relaxation.

Safety is our top priority. We have trained security staff, CCTV surveillance, emergency protocols, counselors for emotional support, and regular safety drills. Parents can track buses through GPS.
